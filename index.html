School Project

We want the children of the poor and backward areas to get quality education just as the children of the better areas get. All Pakistan Muslim Tai Jamat want to open a SCHOOL in a poor area of Lyari to give quality education and enlighten new generation. We believe that getting education is the right of everyone. We want you to please help us in this project.

Clinic Project

All Pakistan Muslim Tai Jamaat wants to set up a clinic in a poor area where poor and low-income people can be provided quality treatment at low cost. An institution where specialist doctors treat people for a low fee. We need your help to complete this project.
